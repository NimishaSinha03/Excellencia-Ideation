Guidelines

Presentation Guidelines

  • Slide 1: Team Introduction

    Include team name, member names, school, and a brief introduction of each member.

  • Slide 2: Problem Statement

    Clearly define the problem your idea aims to solve within the chosen sub-theme.

  • Slide 3: Solution Overview

    Provide a concise overview of your proposed solution. Highlight its uniqueness and relevance to the chosen sub-theme.

  • Slide 4: Technological Innovation

    Explain the technological aspects of your solution. How does it leverage innovative technologies to address the problem?

  • Slide 5: Market Potential

    Present a market analysis outlining the potential impact and reach of your solution. Identify target users and market trends.

  • Slide 6: Implementation Strategy

    Describe your step-by-step plan for implementing the solution. Include key milestones, timelines, and resource requirements.

  • Slide 7: Challenges and Mitigations

    Anticipate and address potential challenges in implementing your idea. Propose effective mitigation strategies.

  • Slide 8: Impact and Future Scope

    Articulate the expected impact of your solution. Discuss its scalability, sustainability, and potential for future developments.

Other Guidelines

Other Guidelines

  • Guideline 1: Format

    1. Submit a single PDF document containing all 8 slides.
    2. Ensure clarity, legibility, and adherence to the provided template.

  • Guideline 2: Slide Content

    1. Each slide should be visually engaging with a balance of text and visuals.
    2. Use bullet points, infographics, and images to enhance clarity.

  • Guideline 3: Presentation Time

    1. Teams will have a maximum of 10 minutes for presentation, including Q&A.

  • Guideline 4: Q&A Session

    1. Be prepared to answer questions from the judging panel. Understand your idea thoroughly.

  • Guideline 5: Criteria

    1. Creativity and Innovation
    2. Problem Definition and Solution Clarity
    3. Feasibility and Implementation Strategy
    4. Market Potential and Impact
    5. Presentation Skills
